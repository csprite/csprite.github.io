csprite (Pronounced see-sprite) is a easy to use pixel art editor, available for a cost of absolutely Free.

The project started as me trying to improve Falconerd's Sixel, but soon enough evolved into much more mature than it's origin. csprite is still very young & while any suggestions or bug-reports are welcomed, It is not recommended to use it as a daily-driver because things can & do break.
